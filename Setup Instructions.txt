Instructions for first time setup of GitHub:


1) Create an account here: https://github.com/join?source=header-home
	- You can use your NU email but feel free to use whatever email account, just remember which one you used.

2) Download this software: https://desktop.github.com/

3) Once you've passed on your email address to the previous owner and downloaded everything, open up the GitHub desktop app and log-in.
	- You can ignore the hint pop-ups.

4) Click on the "+" sign on the top left corner, and select the "Clone" option.

5) Click on the "BME390_WoundExudate" repository, and save it to your "\Documents\GitHub" folder. It should already be the default path for saving.

6) Congratulations! You've successfully setup GitHub on your computer.'
